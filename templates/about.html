TechJobHub is an all-in-one web application designed to streamline recruitment in the tech industry. It simplifies key processes like job posting, application submission, and candidate evaluation, providing a user-friendly experience for both recruiters and job seekers. Recruiters can post job openings, manage applications, and communicate with candidates, while job seekers can build profiles, search for jobs, and apply to relevant positions. Unique features include virtual interviews directly on the platform, custom assessments for candidate shortlisting, and automated skill extraction from resumes. Built with HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and Django, the platform ensures responsiveness, robust data management, and seamless integration. TechJobHub supports scalability with advanced search features, personalized job recommendations, and detailed analytics for employers, all while maintaining industry-leading security and data privacy. The platform is designed to revolutionize the tech recruitment process, fostering connections and empowering career growth in the industry.
